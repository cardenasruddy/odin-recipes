Oven Baked Omelet

Yummy, fluffy eggs made in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 9 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish with butter.
